FAQ

GOT QUESTIONS?
WE'VE GOT ANSWERS.

01 How long does it take to go live?
Most operators go live within 4 weeks from contract signing. This includes brand configuration, game provider setup, payment integration, and back-office training. Complex setups with custom features or licensing requirements may take slightly longer.
02 Do you support crypto payments?
Yes. Flexi supports both crypto and fiat payments out of the box. We integrate with 200+ payment methods including Bitcoin, Ethereum, USDT, and all major fiat processors. Multi-currency wallets and instant withdrawals are included in all plans.
03 Can I use my own brand and domain?
Absolutely. Flexi is a fully white-label platform. You bring your brand, domain, and vision — we handle the technology underneath. Everything from the player-facing UI to the back-office can be customized to match your identity.
04 Which licenses and jurisdictions do you support?
We currently support 40+ markets including MGA (Malta), Curaçao, UKGC, Gibraltar, and Isle of Man. Our compliance engine handles KYC/AML, Responsible Gaming, and geo-blocking automatically. We also assist with new licensing applications.
05 What kind of support do you offer post-launch?
Every operator gets a dedicated account manager from day one. We offer 24/7 technical support, regular performance reviews, and proactive monitoring. Professional and Enterprise plans include SLA-backed uptime guarantees and priority response times.
06 Is there a revenue share model?
Yes. Our Starter plan includes a 5% revenue share, Professional is 3%. Enterprise plans are fully custom and can include flat-fee structures with no revenue share. We're flexible and happy to find what works best for your business model.
